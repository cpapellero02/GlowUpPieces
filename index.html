Our Story

Beauty Born Here

Glow Up Pieces started in a small studio in Cebu City with one big dream — to make premium beauty accessible for every Filipino. We believe that glowing up is not a luxury; it's a right.

From our curated lip collections to our skin-loving foundations, every Glow Up piece is tested on real people, in real Filipino weather, for real results.

Vision

To be the Philippines' most loved and most trusted beauty brand — celebrating every shade, every skin, every glow.

Mission

To create high-performance, inclusive beauty products that are affordable, cruelty-free, and made for the Filipino lifestyle.
